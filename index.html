Meet the house that I grew up in.

Quiet backyard.

So quiet that you could read and sleep out here.

Lots of space.

You could shut the doors and have peace and quiet and there was room for everyone.

Lovely wood floors.

BUT growing up it was a dark green carpet that I had to vacuum every Saturday.

Growing up here was so fun. I loved coming home from school and watching TV in the rec room. It was like being in a cocoon.
